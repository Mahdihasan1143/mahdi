About Ifada Solar

Bangladesh's trusted name in solar energy — manufacturing and delivering clean power solutions since 2006.

Our Story

Nearly two decades of solar leadership

Founded in 2006, Ifada Electronics Ltd. began as a specialist in solar street lighting and has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted manufacturers and suppliers of complete solar energy solutions.

Today we serve homes, businesses, farms and government projects across all 64 districts — combining premium components, expert engineering and dependable after-sales service.

Every product we ship is built with quality parts like LiFePO4 batteries, MPPT controllers and high-efficiency mono panels, and backed by warranty and nationwide support.
