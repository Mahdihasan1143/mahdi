Our Story

About Ifada Solar

Since 2006, Ifada Electronics Ltd. has grown into one of Bangladesh's most trusted manufacturers and suppliers of solar energy solutions. From our beginnings in solar street lighting, we now deliver complete solar systems for homes, businesses, farms and government projects.

Every product is built with premium components — LiFePO4 batteries, MPPT controllers and high-efficiency mono panels — and backed by professional installation and lifelong support. Our mission is simple: clean, reliable, affordable power for every corner of Bangladesh.
